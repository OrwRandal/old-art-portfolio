Who Is Randy?

Hi, my name is Randy. I am 18 years old and I've always had an obsession with anything that enables me to be creative. When I was younger, Even without any skill, I always doodled on my homework and notes. It even caused me to get into trouble a few times, but it never stopped being fun. Every career that piqued my interest, whether it was photography, graphic design, or even now, Software Engineering, has allowed me to create. Art is a way to express yourself and I wanted to create an amalgamation of my interests on this site by creating a site to showcase my art. I hope you enjoy your stay.
